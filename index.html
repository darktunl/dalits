What are Dalits?

Have you ever had a habit, or witnessed the ongoing habit of others, continue to be perpetuated with no end in site? A dalit is essentially taking a stance, calling it out, saying "no more!" Simply put, a dalit is Drawing A Line In The Sand.

This site contains a collection of dalits written from the perspective of the observer with the aim of drawing a line in the sand to end the behavior in question.

Email Etiquette

Stray emails are the equivalent of digital litter. It's not necessary that you reply to every email received with a "Thank You," "Got it" or "Sounds good." If the sender wants confirmation that you've received the email, they can enable various options to track delivery.
